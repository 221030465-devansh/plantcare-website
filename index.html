Plant Care Tips

Basic rules for keeping your indoor and outdoor plants healthy.

💧 Watering 101

Overwatering kills more plants than underwatering. Always check the top 2 inches of soil before watering. If it's moist, wait a few days.

☀️ Light Requirements

"Bright indirect light" means placing your plant near a sunny window, but just out of the direct rays of the sun to avoid scorching the leaves.

✂️ Pruning

Don't be afraid to snip off yellowing or dead leaves. This redirects the plant's energy towards pushing out healthy new growth!
