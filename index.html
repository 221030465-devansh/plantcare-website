Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the PlantCare app.

How accurate is the AI diagnosis? +
Our AI is trained on hundreds of thousands of healthy and diseased plant images. It currently identifies over 50 of the most common plant diseases with an accuracy rate of over 90% when provided with a clear, well-lit photograph.
What kind of photo works best? +
For the best results, ensure the affected leaf or area is well-lit (natural daylight is best) and centered in the frame. Avoid blurry images or photos where the plant is too far away.
Can it identify indoor and outdoor plants? +
Yes! The model is trained to recognize diseases across a wide variety of flora, from common indoor houseplants like Pothos and Ficus to outdoor garden crops like Tomatoes and Apple trees.
